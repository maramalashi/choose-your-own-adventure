Graveyard Shift

You are in need of some extra cash so you look for an extra gig after your full time job. You come across an ad for the night shift at the graveyard right next to your house. You decide to take the job. On your first night working, you get to what looks like the building for the staff.

When you walk in, you are surprised to find no one there. When suddenly, you get a phone call.

You answer the call. "Hello..." It's your boss. He tells you your job is to watch the security cameras and patrol the graveyard every hour.

Oops. It was your boss. He thought you didn't show up for your shift so he fired you.

After an hour, you leave for your first patrol. It's a cool foggy night which sends shivers down your spine. Suddenly, you hear a scream. Do you?...

You check the cameras to find nobody there. You're too scared to leave the building again so you stay inside the rest of your shift. Your boss fires you because you did not patrol. Womp, Womp.

You walk towards the place you heard the scream to find a little girl. The girl runs away from you. Do you?..

You follow her and suddenly fall inside an open grave and hit your head, making you go to sleep. The next morning, one of the other grave workers finds you and thinks that they forgot to close your grave so they bury you alive. Womp, womp. No one knows what became of the graveyard shift employee.

You go back to the cameras to find the little-girl nowhere to be seen. You're too scared to leave the building again so you stay inside the rest of your shift. Your boss fires you because you did not patrol. Womp, womp.
